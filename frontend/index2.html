Neuroguard – Clinical Prognosis Platform

Executive Summary

Description: Neuroguard is an AI-driven clinical prognosis platform for pre- and postoperative monitoring of patients with elevated intracranial pressure (ICP). It processes real-time ICP data, clinical parameters, and alarm signals to provide early warnings and precise survival probability predictions.

Development Status: Fully functional prototype validated with prospective and retrospective patient data, achieving a prediction accuracy of AUC ~0.89.

Key Features:

  • Integration with hospital IT systems
  • Advanced AI analysis using dynamic Deep Learning models: LSTM, Graph Convolutional Networks
  • Visualization of ICP trends and risk progression
  • Explainable AI for clinical interpretability

Target Audience: Universities, neurosurgery departments, research centers, and hospitals seeking cutting-edge patient monitoring solutions.
